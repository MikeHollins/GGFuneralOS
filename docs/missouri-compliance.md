# Missouri Funeral Home Compliance Reference

## Death Certificate Filing

- **System**: MoEVR (Missouri Electronic Vital Records)
- **Deadline**: 5 days from death (RSMo 193.145)
- **Medical certification**: 72 hours from death
- **Funeral director completes**: Demographic section (fields 1-41)
- **Physician/coroner completes**: Medical certification (fields 42-58)
- **Amendable by funeral director via affidavit**: First name, middle name, sex, date of death, SSN, age, DOB, armed forces, place of death, marital status, spouse name, occupation, industry, residence, race, education, parents' names

## Cremation Requirements

- Signed authorization from legal next of kin (required)
- Pacemaker removal (mandatory — explosion risk)
- Some counties require medical examiner sign-off
- 24-hour waiting period before cremation (common practice, check local jurisdiction)

## FTC Funeral Rule (16 CFR Part 453)

- Present General Price List (GPL) at beginning of arrangement conference
- Provide itemized Statement of Funeral Goods and Services Selected
- Casket Price List available upon request
- Outer Burial Container Price List available upon request
- Embalming disclosure: "Embalming is not required by law"
- Violations: up to $50,000+ per incident

## Record Retention (Missouri)

- Death certificates: Permanent
- Case files: Minimum 10 years (recommended permanent)
- Financial records: 7 years
- Pre-need contracts: Permanent until fulfilled + 7 years
