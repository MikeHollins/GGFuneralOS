About Us

Our Staff

Our staff has over (75) seventy-five years of combined service in the funeral industry. Although our profession is servicing our families, we have a commitment to minister to our families, neighborhoods and communities as well.

DiMond Piggie
Chief Operations Officer; Licensed Funeral Director-KS, MO; Licensed Insurance Agent
DiMond Piggie
Chief Operations Officer; Licensed Funeral Director-KS, MO; Licensed Insurance Agent

Mr. DiMond Piggie is a graduate of Norfolk State University with a B.S. in Hotel, Restaurant and Institutional Management and holds a Master of Accounting and Financial Management Degree from Keller Graduate School of Management. He has been a member of the Pi Gamma Chapter of Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Incorporated since 1996. Newly acclimated to the funeral industry, he has whole heartedly served our families with compassion, class and style. His ability to adapt in complex situations and conquer every challenge with poise and integrity, has been second to none.


DiMond is a wonderful father of three young girls and takes much pride in being there for them, just as he is for the families we serve.

Angela D. Husband
Missouri Licensed Funeral Director
Angela D. Husband
Missouri Licensed Funeral Director

Ms. Angela Denise Husband is a native of Chicago, Illinois. She is a licensed Funeral Director in the state of Missouri and serves families with devotion and excellent care. Angela has served in the funeral industry since 1994 and joined our staff of dedicated professionals in 2013.


Angela has been an educator for over thirty five years and a member of Ebenezer Missionary Baptist Church, under the direction of Pastor Jesse Frazier and wife, Shirley Frazier where she serves as the Building Fund Chairman. She is a member of the Missouri-Kansas Funeral Directors & Embalmers Association where she serves as treasurer, the Missouri State Funeral Directors and Embalmers Association, the National Funeral Directors & Morticians Association and the Alpha Kappa Alpha Sorority.
