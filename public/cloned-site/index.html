First, let us say thank you for allowing Golden Gate Funeral & Cremation Services, LLC to serve you at one of the most difficult and vulnerable times in a family’s life, the loss of a loved one. We know that relationships are irreplaceable and that the precious memories you’ve shared are priceless. This is why we at Golden Gate Funeral & Cremation Services, LLC endeavor to build strong ties with our families by supporting those entrusted to us and handling them as our own to ensure that we will do everything possible to “Exchange Your Grief for a Golden Experience”.


We want your tears to be those of joy and not of grief; your cares, free from worry and anxiety; and the financial obligation of this “Golden Event in Time”, to be affordable and free of burden, without sacrificing quality and high class service. We will strive to leave a lasting impression on you and your family, “Golden Memories” that will last a lifetime.


Again, we say WELCOME to the Golden Gate Family. Our thoughts, prayers, love and service are with you always.

How We Can Help

If you've experienced a loss, or are planning in advance, we are here for your each step of the way.
